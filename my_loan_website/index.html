Frequently Asked Questions

How do I qualify for a personal loan and what do I need to apply?

Eligibility factors include financial history, credit history, income and expenses, loan purpose, bankruptcy status, and state of residence. Required documents typically include government-issued ID, Social Security card, proof of residence, and proof of income.

How long does it take to get a personal loan?

The process can be as quick as one day, from application to receiving funds. Online application takes minutes, document signing takes 30-45 minutes, and fund reception varies based on the method chosen.

What's the difference between a secured and unsecured debt consolidation loan?

A secured loan requires collateral (like a vehicle), while an unsecured loan doesn't. Secured loans have specific eligibility requirements and the lender can take the collateral if the borrower defaults.

Are there any fees or charges associated with a personal loan?

Fees may include origination fees, late payment fees, non-sufficient funds (NSF) fees, and governmental fees. The exact amounts vary based on loan terms, state, and loan standing.

Can I pay off my personal loan early without penalties?

With OneMain loans, you can pay off your personal loan early without penalties at any time. You can make a one-time payment for the total outstanding balance or pay extra with each regular monthly payment.
